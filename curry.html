Curry the dish

A curry is a dish with a sauce seasoned with spices, mainly associated with South Asian cuisine. In southern India, leaves from the curry tree may be included. There are many varieties of curry. The choice of spices for each dish in traditional cuisine depends on regional cultural tradition and personal preferences.

Ingredients

  • Curry powder
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Chicken
  • Garlics, shallots, onions

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken w' curry powder for a while
  2. Fry the chicken with garlic, shallot until they turn brown
  3. Add water
  4. Pour all chopped other ingredients into the pot
  5. Add more curry powder and steam for 25 mins
