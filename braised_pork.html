Meatish Browny :v

Vietnamese Caramelized Pork Belly (Thit kho tau) is classic comfort food of Viet families. Pork belly is slowly braised until it has a beautiful caramel color and becomes so flavorful and tender.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Shallot, garlic, pepper, chilis, salt
  • Fish sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the pork belly into chunks
  2. boil the eggs then fry them until they have a blistered and golden crust
  3. Make caramel sauce
  4. Pour everything in and add water
  5. Braise for 50 mins
